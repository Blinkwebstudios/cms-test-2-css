At Blink web studios we found was that a lot of small businesses don't have the funds to spend thousands of pounds upfront on a new website. And the ones that did, got taken advantage of and have a terrible website that looks like it was built by someone in a dungeon and haven't seen design trends for the last 10 years. They were in a frustrating spot - they either can't afford a good website, and if they can they don't know who to trust to make something great. So we tweaked our business model to fit their needs. That's how we came up with our £0 down and £150 a month model. It's more manageable for a small business to handle and won't hurt their bank accounts.
